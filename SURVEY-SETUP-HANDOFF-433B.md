## 📋 Slide field requirements (so widgets find their host + slides don't skip)

Each list slide must contain its native LARGE_TEXT `*_summary` field — the widget hides it and renders itself in its place (keeping the field's wrapper alive so GHL doesn't skip the slide). The summary fields:

`433b__bank_accounts_summary`, `433b__accounts_receivable_summary`, `433b__investments_summary`, `433b__digital_assets_summary`, `433b__available_credit_summary`, `433b__real_property_summary`, `433b__vehicles_summary`, `433b__business_equipment_summary`, `433b__intangible_assets_summary`, `433b__business_liabilities_summary`, `433b__payment_processors_summary`, `433b__credit_cards_summary`, `433b__business_personnel_summary`, `433b__related_party_loans_summary`, `433b__asset_transfers_summary`, `433b__affiliations_summary`.

**Combined slides:** the business-assets slide needs **both** `…business_equipment_summary` **and** `…intangible_assets_summary`; the payment-acceptance slide needs **both** `…payment_processors_summary` **and** `…credit_cards_summary`. (The widget renders both sections together and hides every host field — so its sections must sit on ONE slide, or a later slide would render blank.)

**Digital Assets line 20a** — "individuals with access to keys/wallets" is the **separate single field** `433b__digital_asset_keyholders` (TEXT), NOT part of the repeater. Put it on the digital-assets slide as its own normal field.

## 🧮 Income/Expense/Net calc (Section 5)

`embed-433b-income-expense-calc.js` is a field-anchored module (no `<div>`). Paste it **once** anywhere on the survey; it finds the income/expense/net fields wherever they are and injects a live card on each of their slides, then writes the 3 totals on submit.

- **Income (Line 36)** = sum of the 6 income lines → writes `433b_income__total_monthly_auto` (TEXT).
- **Expenses (Line 49)** = sum of the 12 expense lines (incl. line 48 allowable installment payments) → writes `433b_expense__total_monthly_auto` (TEXT).
- **Net (Line 50)** = Income − Expenses → writes `433b_line_50__net_income_auto` (TEXT).
- The 3 auto-totals **must be TEXT** (GHL drops programmatic writes to currency-masked MONETARY fields). The per-line inputs stay MONETARY (the client types those).
- The `…other_description` fields are plain text — NOT summed.

**Field binding — verify which one resolves (no edit needed either way):** the calc resolves each field by BOTH the GHL customFieldId (`name=`) AND the data-q clean key (`433b_income_-_…`). The Schedule C survey resolved by id; the 433-A intake survey's `name=` was an encrypted blob and only data-q worked. Binding by both means it works regardless — but **open the console on the income slide once** and confirm the totals populate, so you know your survey's behavior.

## 🔑 Lessons baked into the code (same as 433-A)
- **Field matching:** fieldKey `__` → `_-_` in data-q; `name=` may be encrypted → match by data-q (and id) in both forms.
- **Write-back:** GHL React drops `el.value=` → native value setter + input/change events.
- **Slide-skip:** GHL auto-skips a slide whose every form item is `display:none` → the widget keeps the hidden field's wrapper rendered (collapsed).
- **Per-page only, no bundle.** **CDN warming:** jsDelivr can 502 a fresh tag for minutes → use the githack twin.
- **Byte-fidelity:** widget `buildSummary` output is identical to the module's `asset433.ts` (round-trip checked) so a survey-added row and a slide-out-added row are indistinguishable to the downstream parser/PDF.
